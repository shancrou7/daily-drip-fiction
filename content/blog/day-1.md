I woke up in my old childhood room. As I woke up, I saw my sister shaking me, yelling urgently, “PETER! Wake up! Something weird is in the living room.”

I asked, “What do you mean, ‘something weird’?”

She looked at me with a serious face, saying, “Follow me,” and led me to the living room.

To my surprise, I saw something that looked like a person covered in black goo, with no eyes or mouth. Black goo. External evil!

“All right, Lily, go as quickly as you can to Mom and Dad’s room and don’t stop,” I instructed.

“Okay,” Lily responded purposefully.

When Lily left the room, it stared at me, trying to intimidate me with a blank look.
